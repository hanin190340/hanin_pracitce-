A shadowy sorcerer draped in tattered robes feeds on forgotten memories, growing stronger as histories vanish and names are lost.
Elara discovers a scorched parchment bearing half an ancient rune—glowing faintly, pulsing with lost magic, linked to the Chronomancer’s cipher.
Elara meets Squeaky, a time-traveling squirrel in a wizard hat, who speaks in riddles and guards secrets of time itself.
